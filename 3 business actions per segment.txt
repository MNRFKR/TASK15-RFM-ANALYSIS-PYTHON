# Champions (RFM Score 9–12)
- Reward loyalty: Provide exclusive perks such as early access to new products or VIP events.
- Strengthen engagement: Launch referral programs where they earn benefits for bringing in new customers.
- Upsell opportunities: Introduce premium bundles or personalized offers to maximize their already strong spending habits.
# Loyal (RFM Score 7–8)
- Maintain consistency: Offer loyalty points or tiered membership benefits to keep them engaged.
- Personalized communication: Send tailored recommendations based on past preferences.
- Cross-sell campaigns: Introduce complementary products/services to expand their basket size.
# Potential Loyalists (RFM Score 5–6)
- Nurture growth: Provide limited-time discounts or trial offers to encourage repeat purchases.
- Build trust: Share success stories, testimonials, or product guarantees to strengthen confidence.
- Convert to loyalists: Target them with “next purchase” incentives like free shipping or bonus points.
# At Risk (RFM Score ≤4)
- Re‑engagement campaigns: Send win‑back emails with strong incentives (e.g., “We miss you” offers).
- Feedback collection: Ask for input on why they disengaged, then act on those insights.
- Risk mitigation: Offer flexible return policies or customer support outreach to rebuild trust.
